Some of Our Tried and Tested Recipes

Cheesy Egg Omlet

Ingredients:

  • Eggs-4
  • Oil
  • 6 Mushrooms finely chopped
  • Grated Mozarella cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk-1 tablespoon
  • salt and pepper

How to Prepare:

  1. Break the eggs in a bowl and beat them
  2. Add milk to the beaten eggs
  3. In a pan heat oil and butter and fry the chopped mushrooms for 5 minutes
  4. Let the fried mushrooms cool down for 10 minutes
  5. Add the fried mushrooms to the beaten eggs and mix well.Add salt and pepper to this mixture and stir well
  6. Grease a warm pan with some oil and pour the egg mixture onto it. Make sure that the stove is on medium
  7. Close the pan with a lid and let the eggs cook for a few minutes
  8. When the top layer of the omlet is cooked flip it to cook on the other side
  9. Flip the omlet again and spread the grated mozarells cheese on it
  10. When the cheese is melted transfer the omlet onto a plate and serve it hot

Vegetable pulao

Ingredients:

  • Oil-4tbsp, ghee-1tbsp
  • Chopped Onions, potatoes,beans,carrots,greenpeas
  • Dry spices like cinnamon, cardomom, peppercorn
  • cashewnuts and peanuts
  • Basmathi rice-2 cups
  • Pulao masala
  • salt as per taste

How to Prepare:

  1. Heat ghee and oil together in a deep container
  2. Fry the dry spices lightly in the container
  3. Then add the nuts, fry lightly and then add the onions
  4. Once the onions are cooked add all the vegetables
  5. After 5 minutes add the pulao masala and fry for another 5 minutes
  6. Pour 4 cups of water in the container and let it boil.
  7. Meanwhile wash the Basmati rice few times.
  8. Add the Basmathi rice in the container and then add salt.
  9. Let the rice cook until most of the water is absorbed. Close the container with a lid
  10. Lower the heat and let the rice simmer for another 5 minutes until it is soft.
